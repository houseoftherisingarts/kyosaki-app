Privacy Policy

Kyosaki financial integration · Last updated 13 June 2026

Kyosaki ("the App") is a private, single-user financial dashboard operated by 9380-0589 Québec Inc. ("Le Salon des Inconnus") for its own internal bookkeeping. The App is not a public product and has no end users other than the business owner who operates it.

1. Who we are

9380-0589 Québec Inc., 826 Côte-à-Favier, Namur, Québec, J0V 1N0, Canada. Contact: alex@lesalondesinconnus.com.

2. What data the App accesses

When connected to QuickBooks Online through Intuit's official API, the App reads accounting data belonging to the operator's own company, such as account balances, bank transactions, and standard financial reports (profit and loss, balance sheet). It accesses only the data of the company that authorized it.

3. How the data is used

The data is used solely to display the operator's own financial position in a private dashboard. It is never sold, rented, shared, or disclosed to any third party. There is no advertising, profiling, or secondary use of any kind.

4. Storage and security

Financial data and access tokens are stored locally on the operator's own device in a restricted configuration file. Credentials are never transmitted to any server other than Intuit's own API endpoints over an encrypted connection.

5. Data retention and revocation

The operator may revoke the App's access at any time from within QuickBooks Online (Apps settings) or by deleting the local tokens. Doing so immediately ends all data access.

6. Changes

Any change to this policy will be posted on this page with an updated date.
